About Me

"There is always a workaround". That's an ideology I strongly believe in. It's not just something to do with generic hacking, but a reminder that a problem doesn't just have a solution, but a smart solution. I try to find such smart and witty solutions for all puzzles of my life.

I am a passionate practician and an enthusiast of Cyber Security with an MS in Cyber Security from Georgia Tech. With a fortunate touch of mild laziness and witty impulsiveness, I have a unique perspective on tricky problems. Using these skills, I learn more about Cyber Security, about identifying loopholes and thinking about secure designs for different systems.
