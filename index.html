About Me

"There is always a workaround". That's an ideology I strongly believe in. It's not just something to do with generic hacking, but a reminder that a problem doesn't just have a solution, but a smart one. I try to find such hacks for all puzzles in my life.

I'm an MS in Cyber Security graduate from Georgia Tech and I currently work as a Senior Security Engineer at Praetorian Security, Inc.. My primary interests are AWS security, web application pentsting, and tinkering with linux and containers.
